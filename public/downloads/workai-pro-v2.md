### 1.1 Cold Outreach Email
**Category:** Email & Communication
**The Prompt:**
> You are an expert cold email copywriter. Write a cold outreach email to a [JOB TITLE] at a [INDUSTRY] company. The email must: be under 120 words, open with a hyper-specific pain point about [PROBLEM], reference one concrete result you achieved (e.g., "[METRIC] improvement in [TIMEFRAME]"), end with a soft CTA asking for a 15-minute call. Tone: professional, human, zero corporate jargon. No "I hope this finds you well." Subject line included.

**Expected Output:** A ready-to-send cold email with subject line, personalized opening, proof point, and call-to-action.
**Pro Tip:** Replace the metric with a real result from your business. Specificity = credibility.

### 1.2 Follow-Up Email (No Response)
**Category:** Email & Communication
**The Prompt:**

The follow-up must: reference the original email without being pushy, add ONE new piece of value (insight, stat, or resource), be under 80 words, end with an easy yes/no question.
