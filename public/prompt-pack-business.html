Objection Handler Script
"I sell [SERVICE] at [PRICE]. The most common objection I hear is '[OBJECTION]'. Write 3 different response scripts I can use: (1) a logical reframe that uses ROI math, (2) an emotional response that connects to their deeper goal, (3) a takeaway close that creates urgency. Each response must be under 60 words and sound natural in conversation."
